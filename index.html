Research Highlight

VOXEL-GRAPH-VECTOR-RAG
A natural language interface for subsurface digital twins combining hybrid RAG (Neo4j knowledge graph + FAISS dense vector search) with task-specific LLM prompting. Enables conversational querying of 16,116 voxels with 50+ geotechnical attributes each, with results visualized live in Rhino 3D.
